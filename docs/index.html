Your mic,
minus the noise.

Prism is open-source virtual audio middleware for Windows. It sits between your microphone and every app, strips noise out with AI in real time, and shows up as a normal mic in Discord, Zoom, OBS — anything.

What it does

live now

AI noise removal

Fans, keyboards, street noise — removed from under your voice while you talk, not just between words. Two models: RNNoise when you want it feather-light, DeepFilterNet3 when you want it gone. Switch live, dial the strength, watch the meter.

phase 3 · next

Voice isolation

The harder problem: other voices. Roommates, TV, music in the background — separate your voice from theirs so only you make it into the call.

phase 4 · planned

Sound injection

A soundboard wired straight into the mic stream. Hotkeys, per-sound volume, no janky stereo-mix workarounds.
